BearNourish

A Self-Nutrition Kit for UC Berkeley Students

Goal:

BearNourish provides incentivized, nutritional grocery shopping education for UC Berkeley students on a budget. Through an interactive online platform, this resource will teach students how to effectively select products from various food categories, and provide promotional coupons following their program involvement.


Languages Used:

HTML, CSS, & Javascript
