MeNew

An iOS application design for keeping track of your orders at restaurants

Goal:

Usually, when I go to restaurants, cafes, food places in general, I always order the same thing. To overcome this issue, I designed menew. menew is an iOS application designed in Adobe XD to help users be more adventurous with trying different menu items at restaurants.
